About Royale Cafe

Our Story

Established in 2015, Royale Cafe has become Nepalgunj's favorite destination for coffee lovers. Our passion for exceptional coffee, combined with a warm, inviting atmosphere, creates the perfect space for relaxation, work, or social gatherings.

We source the finest coffee beans from local Nepali farmers and complement them with freshly baked pastries, sandwiches, and desserts made daily in our kitchen. Our commitment to quality and customer experience has made us a beloved spot in the community.

Visit us today and experience the Royale difference - where every cup tells a story.
